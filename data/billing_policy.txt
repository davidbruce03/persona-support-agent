BILLING POLICY AND REFUND GUIDE
================================

Last Updated: January 2025

1. SUBSCRIPTION PLANS
---------------------

Free Plan:
- Cost: $0/month
- Features: Up to 1,000 API calls/day, community support, basic analytics
- Limitations: No SLA, no priority support, shared infrastructure

Pro Plan:
- Cost: $49/month (billed monthly) or $39/month (billed annually)
- Features: Up to 50,000 API calls/day, email support (24-48hr response), advanced analytics, dedicated IP
- SLA: 99.9% uptime guarantee

Enterprise Plan:
- Cost: Custom pricing (contact sales@example.com)
- Features: Unlimited API calls, 24/7 phone and email support, dedicated account manager, custom SLA
- SLA: 99.99% uptime guarantee with financial credits


2. BILLING CYCLE
----------------

Billing occurs on the same day each month as your initial subscription date.
For example, if you subscribed on the 15th, you will be billed on the 15th of each month.

Payment methods accepted:
- Visa, MasterCard, American Express
- PayPal
- Bank wire transfer (Enterprise only)
- Invoice billing (Enterprise only, Net-30 terms)

All prices are in USD. International customers may see currency conversion charges from their bank.


3. INVOICE AND RECEIPT POLICY
------------------------------

Invoices are automatically emailed to the billing contact on file within 24 hours of each charge.
To download past invoices:
1. Log in to your account
2. Navigate to Settings > Billing > Invoice History
3. Click "Download PDF" next to any invoice

To update your billing email address:
1. Go to Settings > Billing > Billing Contact
2. Enter new email address
3. Click Save Changes
A confirmation email will be sent to both the old and new address.


4. REFUND POLICY
----------------

Standard Refund Window:
- Refunds are available within 7 days of the initial charge for new subscriptions only.
- Renewal charges are non-refundable except in cases of system errors on our part.

Eligibility Criteria for Refunds:
- First-time subscribers only (not applicable to renewals)
- Account must not have exceeded 10% of plan usage limits
- Request must be submitted within 7 days of charge date

How to Request a Refund:
1. Email billing@example.com with subject line: "Refund Request - [Your Account Email]"
2. Include your account ID, charge date, and reason for the refund
3. Refunds are processed within 5-10 business days to the original payment method

Exceptions (Non-refundable situations):
- Charges older than 7 days
- Accounts that violated our Terms of Service
- Accounts with active disputes or chargebacks
- Add-on purchases (additional API credits, premium features)


5. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
----------------------

If you believe you have been charged incorrectly:
1. First, review your invoice for details on what was charged
2. Check if a team member added seats or upgraded the plan
3. Contact billing@example.com within 30 days of the charge
4. Our billing team will investigate and respond within 3 business days

For urgent billing issues affecting service access, call our billing hotline:
1-800-555-0199 (Mon-Fri, 9AM-6PM EST)

Chargeback Warning:
Initiating a chargeback with your bank before contacting us will result in immediate account suspension.
We strongly recommend contacting us first to resolve any billing discrepancies amicably.


6. PLAN UPGRADES AND DOWNGRADES
---------------------------------

Upgrades:
- Effective immediately upon confirmation
- You will be charged a prorated amount for the remainder of the current billing period
- New limits take effect instantly

Downgrades:
- Effective at the end of the current billing period
- No prorated refund is issued for the unused portion
- Data and configurations are preserved for 30 days after downgrade

Plan Cancellation:
- Cancel anytime from Settings > Billing > Cancel Plan
- Access continues until end of paid period
- Data is retained for 90 days after cancellation for export purposes
- After 90 days, all data is permanently deleted


7. DUPLICATE CHARGE RESOLUTION
--------------------------------

If you see a duplicate charge on your statement:
1. Check if you have multiple accounts using the same payment method
2. Log in and verify under Settings > Billing > Payment History
3. Contact billing@example.com with both charge dates and amounts
4. Our team will investigate within 2 business days
5. Confirmed duplicates are refunded within 5 business days

Common causes of apparent duplicate charges:
- Annual and monthly plan overlap during plan switching
- Failed payment retried successfully on a different date
- Family or team members sharing a payment method across separate accounts


8. SLA CREDITS
--------------

If uptime falls below our guaranteed SLA:
- Pro Plan (99.9%): Credits issued for each hour below SLA at 10% of monthly fee per hour
- Enterprise Plan (99.99%): Credits issued per the custom SLA agreement

To claim SLA credits:
1. File a report at support@example.com within 30 days of the incident
2. Reference the incident ID from status.example.com
3. Credits are applied to the next invoice, not issued as cash refunds
